## MCQ 1
Question: What is a planet generally required to orbit?
A) A moon
B) A star
C) An asteroid
D) A comet
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: How many planets are in our Solar System?
A) Six
B) Seven
C) Eight

Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What are the inner, rocky planets called?
A) Giant planets
B) Gas giants
C) Terrestrial planets
