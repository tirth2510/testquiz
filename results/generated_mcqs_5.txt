## MCQ 1
Question: The word "planet" originates from which language?
A) Latin
B) Greek
C) Roman
D) Egyptian
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: In ancient times, how many "planets" were recognized besides the Sun and Moon?
A) Three
B) Four
C) Five
D) Seven
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What scientific shift led to Earth being recognized as a planet?
A) Geocentrism to Heliocentrism
B) Heliocentrism to Geocentrism
C) The invention of the astrolabe
D) The discovery of Neptune
Correct Answer: A

## MCQ 4
Question: Which of these is NOT a type of planet mentioned in the text?
A) Terrestrial planets
B) Giant planets
C) Gaseous planets
D) Dwarf planets
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 5
Question: Besides Earth, are there other planets known to support life?
A) Yes, many exoplanets support life.
B) Yes, Mars supports life.
C) No, Earth is the only planet currently known to support life.
D) Maybe, scientists are still researching.
Correct Answer: C
